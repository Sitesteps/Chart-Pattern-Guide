Reversal Patterns

Head & Shoulders BEARISH

Three peaks with the middle (head) highest. Break below neckline signals trend reversal at top.

Inverse Head & Shoulders BULLISH

Mirror image at bottoms. Three troughs with middle lowest. Break above neckline signals bullish reversal.

Double Top (M) BEARISH

Price tests resistance twice and fails. M-shape confirms reversal on break below support.

Double Bottom (W) BULLISH

Price tests support twice and holds. W-shape confirms reversal on break above resistance.

Rounding Bottom BULLISH

Gradual U-shaped base over weeks/months. Signals slow accumulation and reversal of downtrend. AKA "saucer."

Triple Top BEARISH

Three failed attempts at resistance. Strong reversal signal — sellers consistently overwhelm buyers at the level.

Continuation Patterns

Cup & Handle BULLISH

U-shaped cup followed by small downward handle. Breakout above rim signals strong upside (O'Neil classic).

Bull Flag BULLISH

Sharp rally (flagpole) followed by tight downward consolidation. Breakout resumes uptrend.

Bear Flag BEARISH

Sharp drop followed by upward consolidation. Breakdown resumes downtrend with similar magnitude.

Ascending Triangle BULLISH

Flat resistance + rising support. Buyers gaining strength. Breakout above resistance typically continues uptrend.

Descending Triangle BEARISH

Flat support + falling resistance. Sellers gaining strength. Break below support extends downtrend.

Symmetrical Triangle NEUTRAL

Lower highs + higher lows converging. Coiling tension. Direction unknown until breakout — trade the break.

Rising Wedge BEARISH

Both lines slope up but converging. Looks bullish but signals exhaustion. Breakdown common — counter-intuitive!

Falling Wedge BULLISH

Both lines slope down but converging. Looks bearish but signals seller exhaustion. Breakout usually upward.

Candlestick Patterns

Doji INDECISION

Open and close nearly equal. Cross-like shape. Market indecision — often a reversal warning at trend extremes.

Hammer BULLISH

Small body at top, long lower wick. Buyers rejected lower prices. Bullish reversal at downtrend bottoms.

Shooting Star BEARISH

Small body at bottom, long upper wick. Sellers rejected higher prices. Bearish reversal at uptrend tops.

Bullish Engulfing BULLISH

Large green candle fully engulfs prior red candle. Strong buying pressure overwhelming sellers.

Bearish Engulfing BEARISH

Large red candle fully engulfs prior green candle. Strong selling pressure overwhelming buyers.

Morning Star BULLISH

Three-candle reversal: big red, small body (doji), big green. Classic bottom signal.

Trend & Momentum Indicators

Golden Cross BULLISH

50-day MA crosses above 200-day MA. Long-term bullish trend confirmation. Major institutional signal.

Death Cross BEARISH

50-day MA crosses below 200-day MA. Long-term bearish signal. Often precedes major downturns.

RSI Overbought CAUTION

RSI above 70 = overbought, potential pullback. Below 30 = oversold, potential bounce. Scale: 0-100.

Bollinger Bands VOLATILITY

Bands expand on high volatility, contract on low. Squeeze often precedes big moves. Price riding upper/lower band = strong trend.

Bullish Divergence BULLISH

Price makes lower lows but indicator makes higher lows. Hidden strength — reversal often follows.

Support & Resistance KEY LEVELS

Levels where price repeatedly bounces. Breaks with volume = significant. Old resistance becomes new support (and vice versa).

Patterns are probabilistic, not predictive. Always confirm with volume and broader market context.
